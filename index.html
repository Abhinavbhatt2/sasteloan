FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting a loan with Saste Loan.

What documents are required for a personal loan?
You need identity proof (Aadhaar/PAN), address proof, income proof (salary slips or ITR for last 2 years), and 6 months' bank statements. For self-employed, GST returns and business proof may also be needed.
How quickly can I get my loan approved?
Personal loans can be approved in as little as 24 hours with complete documents. Home loans and business loans typically take 3–7 working days depending on verification requirements.
What CIBIL score do I need to get a loan?
A CIBIL score of 750+ is ideal for the best interest rates. However, we also work with lenders who cater to scores as low as 650. We help you choose the right lender based on your profile.
Are there any hidden charges?
Never. Saste Loan believes in 100% transparency. All charges — processing fees, insurance (if opted), and any government levies — are communicated clearly before you sign anything.
Can I prepay my loan before tenure ends?
Most lenders allow prepayment after 6–12 months lock-in period. We specifically help you choose lenders with favorable prepayment terms so you can save on interest.
What is the minimum and maximum loan amount?
Personal loans start from ₹50,000 up to ₹40 lakhs. Home loans go up to ₹5 crores, business loans are based on your turnover, and mortgage loans depend on property value. Contact us for your specific requirement.
Is my personal information safe with Saste Loan?
Yes, absolutely. We use bank-grade SSL encryption to protect all your data. Your information is only shared with lenders you choose to apply with, and we never sell data to third parties.
